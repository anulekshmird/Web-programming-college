About the College

The College of Engineering Perumon was established in 2000 by the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE), under the Government of Kerala. The college is located on the scenic banks of Ashtamudi Lake in Kollam district and offers a serene learning environment for aspiring engineers. With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and an excellent academic record, COE Perumon stands as a hub of technical excellence in the region.

Our Vision

To emerge as a centre of excellence in technical education by imparting high-quality education, fostering innovation, and producing socially responsible engineers capable of solving global challenges.

Our Mission

  • Deliver comprehensive technical education with moral and ethical values.
  • Encourage innovation, research, and entrepreneurship among students.
  • Collaborate with industries and institutions for real-world exposure.
  • Create responsible professionals committed to national development.

About CAPE

The Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE) is an autonomous society established by the Government of Kerala in 1999. It aims to provide quality professional education in various fields like engineering, management, and health sciences. CAPE operates several engineering colleges across Kerala, including the College of Engineering Perumon, with the goal of promoting excellence, inclusivity, and innovation in higher education.
